Decentralized Event Ticket Payments

A Solana-based utility payment layer for the event industry. Hosts generate tickets. Guests pay in crypto. No custodial wallets. No fiat handling.

For Event Hosts

Generate ticket batches by defining quantity, price, date and location. Pay generation fees using utility tokens or stablecoins. Receive funds directly to your non-custodial Solana wallet.

For Guests

Pay securely via Solana Pay QR code or your preferred wallet. Receive a payment confirmation and receipt via email. Tickets are delivered separately by the event host.

Payment Assets

Supported assets include community-driven utility tokens and EURC or USDC stablecoins, depending on the event region. Live SOL price conversion is applied at checkout.

Compliance & Privacy

GDPR-aware by design. Minimal data collection. No KYC. No wallet custody. Email data is used solely for payment confirmation.
